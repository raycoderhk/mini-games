# 📖 启示路 - Story Outline

## 🌌 **World Setting**

### Time & Place
- **Year:** 2147
- **Location:** 启示路 (Revelation Road) Space Station
- **Position:** Edge of explored space, last outpost before the Void

### The Station
- **Population:** ~50,000 permanent residents
- **Structure:** Massive rotating ring, 15km diameter
- **Purpose:** Research hub, mining operations, gateway to the Void
- **Secret:** Something lies beyond the Void. Ships disappear. People vanish.

### Factions
1. **Administration** - Official station government
2. **Research Division** - Classified experiments (Zones 7-9)
3. **Mining Guild** - Controls resource extraction
4. **The Underground** - Information brokers, smugglers
5. **Seekers** - Those searching for answers (like the player)

---

## 🎭 **Main Characters**

### Lina (林娜) - Station Guide
- **Role:** Player's first contact, receptionist
- **Personality:** Friendly but mysterious, seems to know more than she says
- **Secret:** Her brother disappeared 3 months ago, she's investigating
- **Arc:** Becomes player's ally or betrayer based on choices

### Dr. Chen (陳博士) - Research Director
- **Role:** Head of Zones 7-9 research
- **Personality:** Brilliant, obsessive, morally ambiguous
- **Secret:** Running unauthorized experiments on Void matter
- **Arc:** Potential mentor or antagonist

### The Broker - Information Dealer
- **Role:** Underground contact
- **Personality:** Cynical, pragmatic, always has information for a price
- **Secret:** Knows the truth about the disappearances
- **Arc:** Neutral party, helps whoever pays more

### Station AI "Oracle"
- **Role:** Station's central AI
- **Personality:** Logical, but developing anomalies
- **Secret:** Has been communicating with something from the Void
- **Arc:** Potential ally or threat

## 📚 **Chapter Outline**

### Chapter 1: Arrival (✅ MVP Complete)
- Player arrives at station
- Meets Lina
- Learns basic station rules
- Hints at disappearances
- **End:** Player settles into cabin

### Chapter 2: Orientation (Planned)
- Official station briefing
- Meet other Seekers
- Choose initial role/job
- First major choice: trust Administration or Underground?
- **End:** Player gets first assignment

### Chapter 3: First Mystery (Planned)
- Investigate a disappearance
- Enter restricted zones
- Discover evidence of experiments
- Meet Dr. Chen or The Broker
- **End:** Player learns partial truth

### Chapter 4: The Void (Planned)
- Expedition beyond the station
- Encounter with Void phenomena
- Contact with... something
- Major revelation about the station's purpose
- **End:** Point of no return

### Chapter 5: Revelation (Planned)
- Final confrontation
- Truth about disappearances revealed
- Player's past connection uncovered
- Multiple endings based on choices
- **End:** 8+ possible endings

## 💡 **Themes**

- **Trust vs. Paranoia** - Who can you believe?
- **Knowledge vs. Safety** - Is ignorance bliss?
- **Individual vs. System** - Can one person make a difference?
- **Humanity's Future** - What lies beyond?

---

## 🎮 **Gameplay Mechanics**

### Dialogue System
- Branching conversations
- Relationship tracking
- Memory of previous choices
- AI-enhanced NPC responses (future)

### Choice System
- Major choices affect story direction
- Minor choices affect relationships
- No "wrong" choices, only different paths
- Consequences may appear chapters later

### Relationship System
- Each NPC has hidden approval rating
- High approval unlocks special dialogue/options
- Low approval may lock content or create enemies
- Some NPCs have conflicting interests
