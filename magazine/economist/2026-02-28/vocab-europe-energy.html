🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This article examines Europe's challenges in transitioning from fossil fuels to renewable energy, including infrastructure costs, political resistance, and the need for massive investment.

中文：這篇文章檢視了歐洲從化石燃料轉型到可再生能源面臨的挑戰，包括基礎設施成本、政治阻力和對大規模投資的需求。

你的句子
transition n /trænˈzɪʃ.ən/ The process of changing from one state to another
n. 轉型，過渡
"Europe's energy transition to renewables"
譯：歐洲向可再生能源的能源轉型
renewable adj /rɪˈnjuː.ə.bəl/ Capable of being replenished naturally
adj. 可再生的
"Renewable energy sources like wind and solar"
譯：風能和太陽能等可再生能源
infrastructure n /ˈɪn.frəˌstrʌk.tʃər/ Basic physical and organizational structures
n. 基礎設施
"Energy infrastructure needs upgrading"
譯：能源基礎設施需要升級
emission n /ɪˈmɪʃ.ən/ The production and discharge of something
n. 排放，排放物
"Carbon emissions must be reduced"
譯：必須減少碳排放
sustainable adj /səˈsteɪ.nə.bəl/ Able to be maintained at a certain rate
adj. 可持續的
"Sustainable development goals"
譯：可持續發展目標
capacity n /kəˈpæs.ə.ti/ The maximum amount that something can contain
n. 容量，能力
"Energy capacity is increasing"
譯：能源容量正在增加
subsidy n /ˈsʌb.sɪ.di/ Money granted by the government
n. 補貼，津貼
"Green energy subsidies are essential"
譯：綠色能源補貼至關重要
fossil fuel n /ˈfɒs.əl ˌfjuː.əl/ Natural fuel formed from organic remains
n. 化石燃料
"Phasing out fossil fuels"
譯：逐步淘汰化石燃料
investment n /ɪnˈvest.mənt/ The action of investing money
n. 投資
"Massive investment in clean energy"
譯：大規模投資清潔能源
target n /ˈtɑː.ɡɪt/ An objective or goal to be achieved
n. 目標，指標
"Climate targets for 2030"
譯：2030 年氣候目標
