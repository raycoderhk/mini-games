🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

President Trump delivered the longest state-of-the-union speech in American history, lasting 1 hour and 48 minutes, outlining his policy agenda and achievements to Congress.

中文：特朗普總統發表了美國歷史上最長的國情咨文演講，歷時 1 小時 48 分鐘，向國會概述了他的政策議程和成就。

agenda n /əˈdʒen.də/ A list of items to be discussed; a plan of action
n. 議程，日程
"Outlining his policy agenda to Congress"
譯：向國會概述他的政策議程
congress n /ˈkɒŋ.ɡres/ The national legislative body
n. 國會，議會
"Addressing a joint session of Congress"
譯：向國會聯席會議發表演講
legislation n /ˌledʒ.ɪsˈleɪ.ʃən/ Laws considered collectively
n. 立法，法律
"New legislation was proposed"
譯：提出了新立法
bipartisan adj /ˌbaɪ.pɑːˈtɪ.zən/ Involving cooperation between two political parties
adj. 兩黨的，跨黨派的
"Bipartisan support for the bill"
譯：法案獲得兩黨支持
economy n /ɪˈkɒn.ə.mi/ The state of a country's wealth and resources
n. 經濟
"The economy is growing steadily"
譯：經濟穩定增長
administration n /ədˌmɪn.ɪˈstreɪ.ʃən/ The executive branch of government
n. 政府，行政部門
"The Trump administration's policies"
譯：特朗普政府的政策
initiative n /ɪˈnɪʃ.ə.tɪv/ A new plan or process to achieve something
n. 倡議，新方案
"A new economic initiative"
譯：新的經濟倡議
reform n/v /rɪˈfɔːm/ To make changes to improve something
n. 改革，改良
v. 改革
"Tax reform was announced"
譯：宣佈稅制改革
budget n /ˈbʌdʒ.ɪt/ An estimate of income and expenditure
n. 預算
"The federal budget was approved"
譯：聯邦預算獲得批准
address n/v /əˈdres/ A formal speech; to speak to
n. 演講，演說
v. 發表講話
"The president's address to the nation"
譯：總統對全國的演講
