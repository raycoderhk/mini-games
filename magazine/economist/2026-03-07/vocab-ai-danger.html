📝 Quick Quiz

Test your understanding of the vocabulary!

1. What does "squabble" mean in this context?

✅ Correct! "Squabble" means a petty argument or quarrel.
❌ Not quite. "Squabble" refers to a petty argument.

2. Which word means "to make less severe"?

✅ Correct! "Mitigate" means to make something less severe.
❌ Not quite. The answer is "mitigate".

3. What does "unprecedented" describe?

✅ Correct! "Unprecedented" means without precedent; never before known.
❌ Not quite. "Unprecedented" means never before known or done.

📚 Grammar Focus

Structure Example from Article Explanation
Modal verbs for possibility "could make", "might lead to" Expressing uncertain outcomes or potential consequences
Passive voice "is being debated", "was signed" Emphasizing the action rather than who performed it
Conditional sentences "If AI systems become autonomous,..." Describing hypothetical situations and their consequences
Complex noun phrases "the disputed contract with the Pentagon" Multiple modifiers before and after the main noun
