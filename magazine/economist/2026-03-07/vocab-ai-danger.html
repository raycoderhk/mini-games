🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This Leaders article discusses the growing tensions between the US government and AI company Anthropic over a disputed Pentagon contract, arguing that this conflict makes AI-related disasters more likely.

中文：這篇領導者文章討論了美國政府與 AI 公司 Anthropic 之間日益緊張的關係，爭議焦點是一份五角大樓合約，文章認為這種衝突使 AI 相關的災難更可能發生。

你的句子
squabble n/v /ˈskwɒb.əl/ A noisy quarrel about something trivial
n. 爭吵，口角
v. 爭論
"The squabble between America's government and Anthropic"
譯：美國政府與 Anthropic 之間的爭吵
disputed adj /dɪˈspjuː.tɪd/ Argued about; disagreed on
adj. 有爭議的，爭論的
"The disputed contract with the Pentagon is worth $200m"
譯：與五角大樓的爭議合約價值 2 億美元
catastrophic adj /ˌkæt.əˈstrɒf.ɪk/ Involving sudden and widespread disaster
adj. 災難性的，毀滅性的
"Catastrophic risks from AI systems are no longer theoretical"
譯：AI 系統的災難性風險不再是理論上的
mitigate v /ˈmɪt.ɪ.ɡeɪt/ Make less severe, serious, or painful
v. 減輕，緩和
"Measures to mitigate AI dangers must be implemented soon"
譯：減輕 AI 風險的措施必須盡快實施
unprecedented adj /ʌnˈpres.ɪ.den.tɪd/ Never done or known before
adj. 史無前例的，空前的
"An unprecedented level of AI capability is approaching"
譯：史無前例的 AI 能力水平正在接近
scrutiny n /ˈskruː.tɪ.ni/ Critical observation or examination
n. 詳細審查，監督
"AI companies are under increased regulatory scrutiny"
譯：AI 公司正面臨更嚴格的監管審查
implications n /ˌɪm.plɪˈkeɪ.ʃənz/ A likely consequence of something
n. 含義，影響，後果
"The implications for national security are profound"
譯：對國家安全的影響是深遠的
autonomous adj /ɔːˈtɒn.ə.məs/ Having the freedom to act independently
adj. 自主的，自治的
"Autonomous AI decision-making raises ethical questions"
譯：自主 AI 決策引發倫理問題
accountability n /əˌkaʊn.təˈbɪl.ə.ti/ The fact of being responsible
n. 問責制，責任
"Questions of AI accountability remain unanswered"
譯：AI 問責制的問題仍未得到解答
trajectory n /tʃəˈdʒek.tər.i/ The path followed by an object; a course of development
n. 軌跡，發展軌道
"AI development is on a dangerous trajectory"
譯：AI 發展正處於危險的軌道上
